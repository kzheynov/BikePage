About us

Production

We strive to produce the highest quality products possible. We produce three different types of bicycles according to the terrain and the needs of the user. Frames are also produced in four sizes with the best possible quality-weight ratio.

Delivery

We will deliver your bicycle as quickly and safely as possible. If we need more time to produce/send it, we'll call you. in case of transport damages, we will send you a new one.

Warranty

We provide a lifetime warranty for the framework and a three year warranty for the components. If you note a cracked frame, we will send you a brand new bicycle.
